ROBOTS: FIRST DAY!!!

July 10, 2017

When you turn on the power, one of the green LEDs lights up. When you press down on a button, the yellow LED lights up. When you press the other button, 2 green LEDs and 1 red LED light up! One problem that I encountered is with the button. The words on the button need to be sideways in order for the circuit to work (AKA the button legs must face outwards towards the sides)! You can put the LEDs in series or in parallel. The button needs to be in series with the LED. The 5 pins are connected horixontally, but in order for the circuit to move vertically, you must connect the pins through a jumper wire, resistor, button, or LED. You mUST use a resistor IN FRONT of an LED, or else the LED will burn out.
